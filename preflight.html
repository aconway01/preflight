Student Solo Endorsement
Current solo endorsement valid for this aircraft category/class and today's conditions. Recorded in logbook (FAR 61.87).
Night Solo Endorsement
Separate night solo endorsement required for solo flight between sunset and sunrise. Must be current and recorded in logbook (FAR 61.93(b)(1)). Required even if departing before sunset if arrival is after sunset.
